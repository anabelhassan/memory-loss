We had two beautiful children, Alex and Michael... or was it Nathan? We would sit by the lake, and he would tell me stories. I remember my husband, Nathan... or was it James? The sun would set, and the laughter of the children filled the air. That was yesterday. Or last week... We had a house by the river, with a garden full of roses... no they were daisies. I remember my husband bringing me flowers every Sunday. Was it Sunday? Time blurs. We would dance in the living room. The radio played our song. What was our song? I can almost hear it... I remember...
